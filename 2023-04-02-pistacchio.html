Pistacchio, a C++ framework

2023, April 02

I’ve been working on a C++ framework for making real-time applications for a while now, and it’s named Pistacchio, which is the Italian word for (a single) pistachio. I know, quite mind blowing.

However, after much studying I finally have something to show. Rise and shine, Suzanne!

It uses SDL2 and OpenGL for OS interaction and displaying graphics. Also, Dear ImGui for the UI, since it’s so easy to plug in and quite pleasant to look at.

My dream is that one day I’ll release the whole thing under an open-source license, but I still have a bunch of things to do (and projects to work on) before feeling comfortable enough to show it to the public…

One of the most interesting things that I’m working on is adding Vulkan support. And another special project that is quite dear to my heart, which I don’t know if it’ll see the light of day (at least fully, I do intend to release a part of it, sooner or later).
